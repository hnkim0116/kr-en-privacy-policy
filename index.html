Privacy Policy — Korean Diary

1. Overview

Korean Diary (“the App”) is a Korean language learning application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

The App does NOT collect, store, or share personal information.

However, the following data may be processed temporarily:

  • Microphone audio for pronunciation practice

3. How the Data Is Used

  • To analyze pronunciation accuracy
  • To provide real-time learning feedback
  • To improve the Korean learning experience

4. Data Storage and Retention

All audio data is processed locally on the device.

  • No cloud storage
  • No server transmission
  • Data is deleted immediately after processing

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use on-device speech recognition provided by the operating system.

No personal data is sent to external servers.

6. Permissions

The App requests the following permission:

  • Microphone — used only for pronunciation practice

You can disable permissions anytime:

Device Settings → Apps → Korean Diary → Permissions

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected on this page.
